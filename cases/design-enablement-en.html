System 02

Design enablement

This page is based directly on your Figma “design enablement” frames. It clearly covers component-library value, AI value, and AI participation across the design lifecycle.

Snapshot

  • Theme: design enablement
  • Focus: component systems, design-to-code, AI collaboration
  • Goal: efficiency gain and lower sunk cost

Value of component-library enablement

  • Product and engineering value: design-to-code mapping reduces review cost; reuse improves build speed; unified language reduces user learning cost.
  • Business value: when new features must launch quickly, a mature component library supports “LEGO-like” rapid development and lowers sunk cost.

Value of AI enablement

  • Efficiency value: self-propagating design systems, AI as a creative copilot, and complaint analysis for pain-point extraction.
  • Business value: faster analysis of large industry reports, human-AI collaborative workflows, and more interactive capability growth.

AI across the full design lifecycle

  • Concept phase: moodboards, financial copy assistance, and AI-supported 5W2H strategy framing.
  • Research phase: complaint clustering, competitive feature matrices, and AI-simulated interviews based on personas.
  • Validation phase: accessibility auditing, AI saliency heatmaps, and AI-assisted A/B test interpretation.
  • Execution phase: token naming and docs generation, realistic financial mock data, and AI-assisted prototype code generation.

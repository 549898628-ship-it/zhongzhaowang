Case Study 04

ASTRO Bank Desktop Redesign

This project was about making a desktop framework capable of handling investment-heavy, multitask workflows rather than only simple banking tasks.

Open Resume Previous Case

Snapshot

  • Role: One of the lead designers
  • Scope: Framework + main interaction layer
  • Focus: desktop investing and multitasking
  • Outcome: 70% implementation efficiency gain

Context

The original desktop framework was suited to relatively simple banking tasks. It struggled when investment workflows and high-frequency multitasking were introduced.

Role and scope

  • One of the key designers responsible for both the desktop framework and main interaction design.
  • Led the end-to-end experience restructuring and cross-platform alignment strategy.

Why redesign the desktop product

  • The original hierarchy and switching cost were too high for investment and multitask scenarios.
  • The product needed to evolve from simple banking support into a desktop environment for heavier investing tasks.

Key decisions

  • Investment capability upgrade: decouple investment workflows into more independent structures while reusing the underlying framework.
  • Unified experience language: cross-platform consistency means users understand the same rules and feedback across devices, not that screens look identical.
  • Desktop value lies in higher information density and more continuous task handling, not only in opening more windows.

Outcome

The Figma content explicitly states that through desktop and mobile experience-language convergence and shared standards, engineering implementation efficiency improved by 70%.

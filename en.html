Case 01

Global Institutional Onboarding and CRM Review System

Role: Lead Designer Scope: Submission + review workflow

Context

Supported institutional onboarding and review across Hong Kong, Singapore, Australia, and the US.

Challenge

  • Heavy documentation and market-specific rules
  • Errors surfaced too late in the process
  • Submission and review experiences were fragmented

Actions

  • Mapped the service blueprint and handoff failures
  • Redesigned submission flow with pre-validation and layered error handling
  • Rebuilt the reviewer workspace around priority and exception detection

Outcome

  • First-pass approval rate improved from 62% to 89%
  • Rejection rate reduced by 40%
  • Review efficiency improved by 35%
